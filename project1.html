Getting Started

After countless projects, tutorials and playgrounds, I decided that it was time to organzie all of my work in a single place to be viewed.

Background

In my quest to learn what makes an optimal portfolio, I took to reviewing countless portfolios from designers and programmers and talking with those in the industry, I began to shape how I best thought to present my work.Through implementing a clear, concise and story-like structure to the way in which I crafted each project, I believe the viewer will not only determine for themselves, my ability as a programmer and designer, but also enjoy the process.
